04 — Brand Voice

Smart, direct,
never hype.

RevenueZen speaks like the most capable senior strategist in the room — confident, specific, and focused entirely on outcomes. No buzzword soup. No empty confidence. Every claim earns its keep.

01
Expert, Not Academic
Write like someone who does the work, not someone who studies it. Practitioners over professors. Real case examples over theoretical frameworks.
02
Specific, Not Vague
Specificity builds trust. "We improved organic traffic" is weak. "We drove 284% more pipeline-attributed sessions in 6 months" is the standard.
03
Helpful, Not Salesy
Every content asset should deliver genuine value before it asks for anything. Teach, then convert. The best sales page is one that didn't feel like a sales page.
✓ Do This
→Lead with the business outcome, then explain how
→Use specific numbers when available ("384% avg traffic growth")
→Write in second person: "your pipeline," "your buyers"
→Name the problem before presenting the solution
→Be technically credible — assume a sophisticated B2B marketer
→Short sentences for impact. Longer sentences for context and nuance.
→Use active voice at all times
✕ Don't Do This
✕Don't say "cutting-edge," "game-changing," or "revolutionary"
✕Avoid passive voice: "results can be seen" → "you'll see results"
✕No corporate-speak: "leverage synergies," "holistic approach"
✕Don't overpromise. Show receipts instead.
✕Avoid exclamation marks in marketing copy
✕Don't open emails or blogs with "In today's fast-paced..."
✕Never start a headline with a question that has an obvious answer
05 — Personas

Three readers.
Every piece.

We write for B2B SaaS companies at $5M–$100M ARR: founder-led or recently hired revenue leadership, with in-house marketing but limited SEO expertise. Every piece of content should speak to at least one of them.

Persona 01
VP / Head of Marketing
  • Cares about pipeline contribution, not vanity traffic
  • Skeptical of "SEO hacks"
  • Wants repeatable systems and predictable growth
Write to their
Show systems, not tactics. Lead with pipeline impact and repeatable results backed by real numbers.
Persona 02
Founder / CEO
  • Time-constrained. Gets to the point fast.
  • Wants to understand why before buying
  • Looks for partners, not vendors
Write to their
Earn trust fast. Lead with the why before the what. Respect their time in every sentence.
Persona 03
Revenue / Growth Leader
  • Thinks cross-functionally: sales + marketing + RevOps
  • Interested in attribution, buyer intent, deal velocity
  • Hates fluff and surface-level advice
Write to their
Go deep. Use hard stats. Acknowledge trade-offs. They will spot surface-level thinking immediately.
They already know
  • +SEO basics and content marketing fundamentals
  • +B2B funnels and pipeline thinking
  • +The emerging importance of GEO and AIO
  • +Why most SEO content underperforms
They're looking for
  • →Strategic clarity, not basic explainers
  • →Real-world application with statistics and hard facts
  • →Trade-offs and nuance, not absolutes
  • →Someone who makes them feel understood

# RevenueZen Brand System

## 1. Colors
| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Midnight | #080E18 | Page background |
| Deep Navy | #111E30 | Cards, elevated surfaces |
| Navy Mid | #1A2D44 | Borders, dividers |
| RZ Dark Blue | #123255 | Primary brand, nav |
| RZ Light Blue | #1CA1FA | Links, highlights, trust signals |
| RZ Green | #0DE14E | CTAs, accent, growth indicators |
| Ice White | #F0F7FF | Primary text on dark |
| Sky Cream | #E8F2FC | Light backgrounds, print |

## 2. Typography
| Style | Font | Size | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display/H1 | Montserrat | 64-96px | 800 |
| H2 | Montserrat | 40px | 700 |
| H3 | Montserrat | 24px | 600 |
| Body | Montserrat | 16px | 400 |
| Label | DM Mono | 11-13px | 400-500 |

Rules:
- Max 3 weights per page (400 body, 600 UI, 700-800 display)
- Tight tracking on large type: -0.03em to -0.05em at 36px+
- DM Mono labels always uppercase, letter-spacing 0.08-0.12em
- Line-height: Display 0.9-1.0 / Headings 1.05-1.15 / Body 1.65-1.75

## 3. Brand Voice
Personality: Smart, direct, never hype. Experienced but not arrogant. Every claim earns its keep.

DO:
- Lead with the business outcome, then explain how
- Use specific numbers: "384% avg traffic growth" not "significant growth"
- Write in second person: "your pipeline," "your buyers"
- Name the problem before presenting the solution
- Short sentences for impact. Longer for context and nuance.
- Active voice at all times
- Contractions encouraged

DON'T:
- No em dashes. Ever. Use commas or periods instead.
- No passive voice
- No hype: "cutting-edge," "game-changing," "revolutionary"
- No corporate-speak: "leverage synergies," "holistic approach"
- Don't overpromise. Show receipts instead.
- No exclamation marks in marketing copy
- Don't open with "In today's fast-paced..."

BANNED WORDS: unlock, leverage (verb), game-changing, revolutionary, best-in-class, crush your competition, lead gen, SEO domination, conversions, revenue acceleration, cutting-edge, holistic

POSITIONING:
- Always GEO (never "AI SEO" or "LLM SEO")
- Always Pipeline as primary outcome (not just "leads" or "traffic")
- Always SME-led for expert content (not "AI content")

## 4. Blog Structure Rules
- Always include TL;DR at the top
- Always include 3-5 specific FAQs at the end
- Clear H2s and H3s throughout
- Max 2-4 lines per paragraph
- Bullet points for scannability
- US English, contractions encouraged

## 5. Target Personas
Writing for B2B SaaS companies at $5M-$100M ARR.

Persona 01 - VP/Head of Marketing:
- Cares about pipeline contribution, not vanity traffic
- Skeptical of SEO hacks
- Write to: Show systems not tactics. Lead with pipeline impact and real numbers.

Persona 02 - Founder/CEO:
- Time-constrained, wants to understand why before buying
- Looks for partners not vendors
- Write to: Earn trust fast. Respect their time in every sentence.

Persona 03 - Revenue/Growth Leader:
- Cross-functional thinker (sales + marketing + RevOps)
- Hates fluff and surface-level advice
- Write to: Go deep. Use hard stats. Acknowledge trade-offs.

## 6. How to Apply
Copy: Direct, outcome-led, active voice. No banned words. Second person. GEO/Pipeline/SME-led positioning.
HTML/CSS: Dark navy backgrounds (#080E18, #111E30). Montserrat headlines. DM Mono labels uppercase. RZ Green (#0DE14E) for CTAs.
Data viz: Navy palette. Montserrat headlines. DM Mono axis labels. RZ Green as primary accent. No gradients.
